ALL ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Saroj and I am 22 years old. I am originally from Jhapa, a beautiful district in the eastern region of Nepal. Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology (BIT) in Kathmandu. I have been studying in the capital city since 2018 and have been thoroughly enjoying my experience here.

I have always been interested in technology and its endless possibilities, which led me to choose BIT as my academic field. I am passionate about learning new programming languages and exploring the latest trends in the tech industry. During my studies, I have had the opportunity to work on various projects and gain practical experience in software development, web development, and database management.

I am also a firm believer in the existence of God. I find solace in prayer and meditation, and I believe that spirituality plays a crucial role in shaping one's perspective and outlook towards life.
